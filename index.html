OrganoChem — The Complete Organic Chemistry by Subrat Biswas

Subrat Biswas is a Research Scientist in the API Research and Development division at Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd., with over four years of experience in synthetic organic chemistry and process development. His work focuses on API synthesis, impurity profiling, and scale-up of chemical processes, with a strong emphasis on regulatory compliance and efficient drug development.
